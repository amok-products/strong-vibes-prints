# Code of Conduct

This is a small open-source project. Keep it simple:

- Be respectful and constructive, and assume good intent.
- Keep discussion focused on the project — code, docs, ideas, and bugs.
- No harassment, personal attacks, or spam.

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, and may block anyone who does not
follow these guidelines. Concerns? Contact the maintainers privately via the
channel in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md).
