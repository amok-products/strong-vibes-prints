reference code repositories are licensed under **Apache-2.0**. Neither license
grants trademark rights — this document covers the names. Together they
implement the program's intent: **the specs and code are free to use, including
commercially — but the names travel with accreditation.**

## What the licenses give you

Under **CC-BY-4.0** (this repo's specs and docs) you may share and adapt the
material, including commercially, provided you give attribution as the license
requires. Under **Apache-2.0** (the reference code repositories) you may use,
modify, distribute, and commercialize the code, subject to that license
(including preserving the `NOTICE` file). CC-BY-4.0 and Apache-2.0 both stop
short of granting trademark rights — this document states how the names may be
used.

## Grant to use the names

You **may**:

- Use the specifications and code freely under their respective licenses.
- Make **factual compatibility / interoperability statements**, e.g.
  "implements the Strong Vibes Protocol by europemagicwand.com" or
  "compatible with Strong Vibes Connect" or "works with Europe Magic Wand®
  devices", provided the statement is truthful and does not imply endorsement.
- Keep a "Strong Vibes" product name **if** you preserve the accreditation below.

You **must**, when using a "Strong Vibes" name to identify a product or a fork:

- Use the accreditation form **"Strong Vibes X by europemagicwand.com"**
  (for example, "Strong Vibes Playapp by europemagicwand.com"), preserving the
  `by europemagicwand.com` credit.
- Preserve the required attribution (CC-BY-4.0 attribution for specs/docs; the
  `NOTICE` file for code) in distributions.
- Attach the ® symbol only to "Europe Magic Wand®" (the registered mark), where
  the parent product is named. Never attach ® to any "Strong Vibes" mark — use ™
  where a mark is shown. This does not waive the accreditation form required
  above.

You **may not**:

- Present a modified or non-conforming fork as the official project, or imply
  endorsement by AMOK / Europe Magic Wand.
- Claim any "Strong Vibes" mark as a registered trademark.
- Use the marks in a way that is misleading or disparaging.

## Non-conforming forks

AMOK / Europe Magic Wand **reserves the right to require a fork that does not
conform to this policy to remove or change the "Strong Vibes" and "Europe Magic
Wand®" names** (i.e. to rebrand). This does not affect your rights to the specs
or code themselves under their licenses — only the use of the names.
